About

Learn more about us

Independent Artist NFT Publisher

Welcome to Decentral CORP NFT Group Publishing DC|NFT|GP the Planets leading-edge NFT media and entertainment company.

Our work is to enliven the Arts by empowering Creators through Blockchain to connect with their audiences better & be paid via cryptocurrency in realtime.

We are a group of developers that are committed to open source solutions and innovation.

We are creating a catalogue of music, art, fashion & film that will bring value to not only our Artists but also the Fans that purchase our NFTs.

We are re-envisioning the music, art, fashion & film industries. Decentral CORP NFT Group Publishing DC|NFT|GP is an American NFT TRC-721 Publishing House owned entirely by parent company Decentral CORP, a subsidiary of Crestonics Corporation. It was formed in 2020 by Robert Campbell with funding from Blockchain entrepreneurs and enthusiasts. It began as a niche market publisher, and is expanding as the NFT niche becomes the industry standard.
